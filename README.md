# the-southern-vietnamese-echoes

A digital sanctuary dedicated to the lyrical music of Southern Vietnam before 1975 and the enduring works of the diaspora. Beyond just melodies, we preserve the soul of a generation through the stories behind every song.

## What you'll find here
- Curated collections of classic Southern Vietnamese tracks.
- Archival context that highlights the people, places, and moments behind the music.
- A welcoming space for listeners to explore and remember the region's cultural heritage.

> DISCLAIMER: THIS APPLICATION IS DEDICATED SOLELY TO PRESERVING THE BEAUTY OF ART AND MUSIC. WE ARE NOT AFFILIATED WITH ANY POLITICAL MOVEMENTS AND DO NOT PERMIT POLITICAL DISCUSSIONS TO INTERFERE WITH THE CONTENT. PLEASE MAINTAIN A RESPECTFUL ATMOSPHERE FOR ALL USERS.

## How to contribute
We welcome respectful contributions that help surface new stories, memories, and recordings. Please share sources whenever possible so that future listeners can trace the origins of each piece.
